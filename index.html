Welcome to the Top 13 Best Pokemon (plus 1)

#13 Jigglypuff

A dual-type Normal/Fairy Pokemon

Jigglypuff uses its eyes to mesmerize opponents,and then sing a soothing, mysterious lullaby (adjusting the wavelengths of its voice to match the brain waves of its foe) to make its opponent fall into a deep sleep.

#12 Decidueye

A dual-type Grass/Ghost Pokemon

Decidueye can mask its presence and attack unaware opponents. It is extremely quick to attack and accurate, capable of striking targets over half a mile away.

#11 Missing No.

A dual-type Bird/Normal Glitch Pokemon

MissingNo's powers and abilities are varied, such as elemental manipulation, reality warping, poison, telekinesis and more. But, worst of all, it has the cataclysmic potential to crash the game and destroy multiple universes.

#10 Garchomp

A dual-type Dragon/Ground Pokemon

With amazing ability to absorb tons of damage, superspeed flying skills, and powerful attacks that can deal heavy damage to multiple opponents at once, Garchomp is a formidable Pokemon with the best of all worlds!

#9 Greninja

A dual-type Water/Dark Pokemon

He moves with the speed and grace of a ninja! His abilities to compress water to make weapons powerful enough to slice through metal is what made Greninja 2020's Pokemon of the year.

#8 Mimikyu

A dual-type Ghost/Fairy Pokemon

Using a disguise to make friends, it is rumored that seeing its true form will cause illness and/or a painful death...the reason Mimikyu will violent attack anyone who attempts to see beneath its cloak.

#7 Blaziken

A dual-type Fire/Fighting Pokemon

Blaziken uses the energy from the fire it generates to increase power and speed. Its fire attacks are strong, but its flurry of savage kicks will end any opponent.

#6 Gengar

A dual-type Ghost/Poison Pokemon

Mischievous and malicious, Gengar delights in its victims' terror. It enjoys playing pranks and casting curses, is highly accurate in its dark attacks, and is very loyal to trainers that treat it well.

#5 Rayquaza

A dual-type Dragon/Flying Legendary Pokémon

In addition to its ability to negate all weather effects and unleashing power beams of energy at its opponent, it is the only Pokemon with the Dragon Ascent ability, the most powerful Flying-type move in existence!

#4 Charizard

A dual-type Fire/Flying Pokemon

Heart and honor - never attacking weaker opponents. Its fire burns hotter with more experience, intensifies to blue if irritated enough, and capable of melting boulders and glaciers, and remaining lit under water.

#3 Suicune

A Water-type Legendary Pokémon,

With the power to walk across water and purify dirty water with one touch, it is believed to be the embodiment of both the north winds and compassion of pure spring waters.

#2 Pikachu

An Electric-type Pokemon

It stores electricity in the electric sacs on its cheeks. When it releases pent-up energy in a burst, the electric power is equal to a lightning bolt.

#1 Mewtwo

A Psychic-type Legendary Pokémon

Capable of levitation, telekinesis, telepathy, mind control, and the ability to create powerful storms, Mewtwo is arguably one of the most powerful Pokemon of all.
